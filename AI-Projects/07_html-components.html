28. 🍲 Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken - 500g
  • Rice - 2 cups
  • Onions - 2 medium
  • Tomatoes - 2
  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Biryani masala - 2 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices for 30 mins.
  2. Fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Cook chicken until tender.
  4. Boil rice with whole spices.
  5. Layer rice and chicken, cook on low flame for 20 mins.

Prep time: 30 mins | Cook time: 45 mins | Serves: 4

Tags: Pakistani, Non-veg, Spicy
